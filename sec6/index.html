User Manual

Encryption:

  1. Choose Algorithm (AES, DES ..)
  2. Enter your secret key (AKA password)
  3. Copy/paste or write your text you want to encrypt
  4. Click Encrypt
  5. Click on Download to save your file

Decryption:

  1. Choose Algorithm (AES, DES ..)
  2. Enter your secret key you used for encryption (AKA password)
  3. Copy/paste or write your encrypted text you want to decrypt
  4. Click decrypt
  5. Click on Download to save your file

You can use Hash function (SHA1, SHA3, MD5) to check the integrity of your text in the future.
